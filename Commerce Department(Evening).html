🏛️ About the Department

The Commerce Department (Evening) was established in 1960. From the beginning, it has offered B.Com Honours in Accounting & Finance and B.Com General courses.

Currently, the department follows the CCF system under the University of Calcutta curriculum. It is the largest department of Bidhan Chandra College, regularly producing several first-class results in university examinations.
